Welcome to my personal webpage. I am a postdoctoral fellow of the Pacific Institute of Mathematical Sciences at the University of British Columbia, in Canada. I work under the guidance of Young-Heon Kim, Brendan Pass and Dave Schneider, my goal is to use optimal transport to study the shapes of dendritic structures.

I have completed a PhD in mathematics under the supervision of Filippo Santambrogio at Université Paris-Sud, in Orsay. The defense took place on the 24th of May, 2019.
