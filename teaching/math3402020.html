Math 340 Introduction to linear programming: Monday Wednesday Friday 12pm-1pm in BUCH A201. Outline of the course.

Office hours are: Wednesday 1pm-2pm in MATH 105 and Thursday 4:30pm-5:30pm in MATX 1118.

Lecture notes

The instructor in charge is Richard Anstee, he posts lecture notes, practice quizzes, and more here. In particular, you can find in this document the theorems about LP and duality that you need to know.

In addition to Anstee's lecture notes, you can find below additional material.

  • How to perform a pivot?
  • The Two Phase method.
  • Why does the simplex algorithm work?
  • What does complementary slackness look like in practice?
  • The revised simplex formulas and their proof.
  • The economic significance of dual variables: an example.
  • Reminders about matrix manipulations.
  • Degeneracy and its consequences.
  • Comments on the implementation of the simplex algorithm.
  • How to check optimality of a basis?
